Overview & Repertoire

    Symphony of Voices 2015 was held on 17 May 2015, 7:30pm at the Esplanade Concert Hall. The theme for this year was Aurora Borealis.

Repertoire

  • FIRST HALF
  • There Is Sweet Music Here by Z. Randall Stroope
  • Stopping By Woods On A Snowy Evening by Ruth Artman
  • Trees by Oscar Rasbach
  • Full Fathom Five by R. Vaughan Williams
  • Linden Lea by R. Vaughan Williams
  • 刺 (Ci) by Zechariah Goh
  • 猜调 (Cai Diao) by Zechariah Goh
  • Itsuki no Komori uta by Ko Matsushita
  • Karimatanu Kuicha by Ko Matsushita
  • SECOND HALF
  • Chant to Bring Back the Wolf and Chant to Make the Magic Work by R. Murray Schafer
  • Snowforms by R. Murray Schafer
  • Guitarra by Stephen Smith
  • Lux Aurumque by Eric Whitacre
  • O Nata Lux by Guy Forbes
  • Aglepta by Arne Mellnas
  • De Circuitu Aeterno by Petr Eben
  • Pseudo Yoik by Jaakko Mäntyjärvi
  • Northern Lights by Ēriks Ešenvalds
  • Yagi Bushi by Ko Matsushita (encore piece 1)
  • Entreat Me Not To Leave Youby Dan Forrest (encore piece 2)
