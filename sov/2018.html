Overview & Repertoire

    Symphony of Voices 2018 was held at the Esplanade Concert Hall at 7:30pm, on 23rd May 2018. The theme for this year was All Things Bright and Beautiful.

Repertoire

  • FIRST HALF
  • Lux Aeterna by Ēriks Ešenvalds
  • Malagueña by Einojuhani Rautavaara
  • Wandering Heart by Ēriks Ešenvalds
  • Star of the County Down arranged by Ben Parry
  • She Walks In Beauty by Seow Mao Yu (lyrics by George Gordon Byron)
  • The Cloud by Toh Xin Long (lyrics by Sara Teasdale)
  • Leonardo Dreams Of His Flying Machineby Eric Whitacre
  • SECOND HALF
  • Phoenix by Ola Gjeilo
  • Among The Leaves So Green, O by John Byrt
  • The Teddy Bear's Picnic by John W. Bratton
  • Unicornis Captivatur by Ola Gjeilo
  • Double, Double Toil and Trouble by Jaakko Mantyjarvi
  • The Goslings by Frederick Bridge
  • The Mermaid arranged by John Whitworth
  • Africa by David Paich and Jeff Porcaro (arranged by Phillip Lawson)
  • The Circle of Life by TBA (encore piece)
