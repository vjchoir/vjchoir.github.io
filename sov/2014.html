Overview & Repertoire

    Symphony of Voices 2014 was held on 26 May 2014, in the Esplanade Concert Hall. The theme for this year was Asian.

Repertoire

  • FIRST HALF
  • Pastime with Good Company by Henry VIII
  • Die mit Tränen säen by Heinrich Schütz
  • Crucifixus by Antionio Lotti
  • Io Piango by Morten Lauridsen
  • Ciao, Bella Ciao by Ben Parry
  • The Battle of Jericho by unknown
  • Alleluia by Ralph Manuel
  • O Nata Lux by Guy Forbes
  • The Spheres by Ola Gjeilo
  • SECOND HALF
  • Japanese Game by Ko Matsushita
  • Itsuki no Komori Uta by Ko Matsushita
  • 双城恋曲 (Love in Two Cities) by Nelson Kwei
  • 沂蒙山歌 (Yi Meng Shan Ge) by 张以达 (Zhang Yida)
  • Reminiscences of Hainan by Dr. Zechariah Goh Toh Chai
  • Dayo Dayo Kupita by Nilo Alcala
  • Naiman Sharag by Se Enkhbayar
  • Meplalian by Budi Susanto Yohanes
  • Yamko Rambe Yamko by Agustinus Bambang Jusana
  • All My Heart This Night Rejoices by Z. Randall Stroope (encore piece)
