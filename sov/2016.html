Overview & Repertoire

    Symphony of Voices 2016 was held at the Esplanade Concert Hall at 7:30pm, on 25th May 2016. The theme for this year was Sands of Time.

Repertoire

  • FIRST HALF
  • Hallelujah by George Frideric Handel (edited by Walkins Shaw)
  • Ne Timeas Maria by Tomás Luis de Victoria
  • Abendlied by Josef Rheinberger
  • Rest by R. Vaughan Williams
  • Tomorrow Shall Be My Dancing Day by Philip Stopford
  • Unicornis Captivatur by Ola Gjeilo
  • Te Deum by Franz Joseph Haydn
  • SECOND HALF
  • 可爱的山乡 (The Lovely Mountain Village) by Leong Yoon Pin
  • 唱起山歌岭游 (Mountain Voyage of Folk Songs) by Leong Yoon Pin
  • 思念 (Nostalgia) by Leong Yoon Pin
  • 街頭巷尾 (Street Calls) by Leong Yoon Pin
  • The Heaven's Flock by Ēriks Ešenvalds
  • Rivers of Light by Ēriks Ešenvalds
  • The New Moon by Ēriks Ešenvalds
  • Amazing Grace by Ēriks Ešenvalds
  • Northern Lights by Ēriks Ešenvalds (encore piece)
