Overview & Repertoire

    Symphony of Voices 2017 was held at the Esplanade Concert Hall at 7:30pm, on 15th May 2017. The theme for this year was Across the Vast Eternal Sky.

Repertoire

  • FIRST HALF
  • I Carry Your Heart by Connor Koppin
  • Nader My God By U by André van der Merwe
  • Let Thy Love Play Upon My Voice by Aldo Joson
  • Pater Noster by John August Pamintuan
  • Je Suis Déshéritée by Pierre Cadéac
  • A Basque Lullaby by Dan Forrest
  • Plenty Good Room by Kirby Shaw
  • Only In Sleep by Ēriks Ešenvalds
  • Long Road by Ēriks Ešenvalds
  • SECOND HALF
  • Sure On This Shining Night by Morten Lauridsen
  • La Luna by Z. Randall Stroope
  • We Beheld Once Again the Stars by Z. Randall Stroope
  • De Circuitu Aeterno by Petr Eben
  • Starlight by Keith Thomas (arranged by David Maddux)
  • I Sing The Body Electric by Ed Lojeski
  • Iuppiter by Michael Ostrzyga
  • Across the Vast Eternal Sky by Ola Gjeilo
  • Let My Love Be Heard by Jake Runestad (encore piece)
