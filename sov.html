Welcome to the Symphony of Voices page!

Hey there! Welcome to the landing page for Symphony of Voices.

Symphony of Voices is the Victoria Junior College Choir's annual concert, held usually at the end of the J2 batch's stint in choir. Each concert has its own theme, with a variety of songs associated with the theme being sung in the concert, usually in the second half.

This part of the website contains information for each Symphony of Voices, with their repertoire and recordings stored and available for download here.

Contributing:
As with other pages, there is a lot you can contribute here. If you're able and willing to provide any details regarding your own SOV, whether it is available here or not, please head over to the Contribute page to find out how you can help with this section of the website. We really appreciate any contributions you can make to help the archive be better :)
