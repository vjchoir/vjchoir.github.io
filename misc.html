Frequently Asked Questions!

Q: How did you make the website?

A: It's a combination of HTML, CSS and Javascript. HTML tells your browser what you wanna show, CSS does the design and Javascript does the coding magic for the music player and stuff.

Q: Can we get our music on Spotify / Apple Music?

A: Technically yes, but there is a lot of red tape in the way. You need to get the permission of every single composer and their record labels or whatever which costs money and I do not have money lmao.

Q: Why are there no videos for some years?

A: Those years the videos have been lost to other archives not as well maintained as this one probably. A lot of the older videos and recordings are probably with the infocomm society of VJ that I'm unable to contact. (if you do be able please do contact me I appreciate it a lot)

Q: How long will you maintain this site?

A: I have no idea. This is like some sort of passion project for me and it has been like that for awhile. So has jumpcutfindo. There's some sense of fulfilment I guess when I do this so I'll keep doing it until I don't feel like it.
