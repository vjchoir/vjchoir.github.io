Overview & Repertoire

    Symphony of Voices 2013 was held on 21 May 2013 at the Esplanade Concert Hall. The theme for this year was Victorian.

Repertoire

  • FIRST HALF
  • Magnificat by Giles Swayne
  • Phoenix by Ola Gjeilo
  • Ronde by Maurice Ravel
  • Yunemita Monowa by Kohei Tanaka
  • Lay a Garland by Robert Pearsall
  • Lux Aurumque by Eric Whitacre
  • Nox Aurumque by Eric Whitacre
  • Magnificat Gloria by Alberto Gran
  • SECOND HALF
  • It Was A Lover and His Lass by Thomas Morley
  • Flora Gave Me Fairest Flowers by John Wilbye
  • Greensleeves by R. Vaughan Williams
  • Dashing Away With the Smoothing Iron by John Rutter
  • The Oak and the Ash by Gordon Langford
  • She's Like A Swallow by Edward T. Chapman
  • O My Love is Like a Red, Red Rose by Simon Carrington
  • Nae Luck About the House by Gordon Langford
  • Distant Land by John Rutter
  • Bobby Shaftoe by Gordon Langford (encore piece)
